About NCFSE -

Fire & Safety Engineering

NCFSE is one of the best institutions in the field of fire Engineering and Safety Management. NCFSE is conducting various courses in Diploma, Advanced Diploma and Post Graduate Diploma programs in the field of Industrial / General Safety, Fire Engineering, Health and Environment Safety and Disaster Management. The courses offered by NCFSE in collaboration & joint certification with IIE, An Autonomous Organisation of the Ministry of skill Development and Enterpreneurship, Govt of India.

This MOU, Collaboration & Joint Certification will provide the students and unemployed youth a viable career building opportunity in the field of Fire & Safety, henceforth fulfilling the Vision of NCFSE & IIE simultaneously.
