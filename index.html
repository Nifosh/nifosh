About NIFOSH:

National Institute of Fire, Occupational Health and Safety

The NIFSE was established under government registrations Act, Nagpur and running training campus at Mecosabagh Methodist Church, Kadbi chowk, Mecosabagh, Nagpur, over an area of 5 acres of land, four kms away from Nagpur Railway Station. All courses provided under National Institute of Fire and Safety Engineering, are recognized by Ministry of Labour and Employment (Govt. of India), University Grant Commission, (Govt. of India), Technical Board and AICTE - UGC - DEC joint committee for Advance Courset, Degree Course, Post Graduate, Diploma Courses in Fire Safety, Industrial Safety Engineering, and Security Management, where pupils can train themselves into excellent Fire Safety Engineers and Safety Officers.
